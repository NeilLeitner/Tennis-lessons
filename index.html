About Me

Hello! I’m Neil Leitner, a passionate tennis instructor with over 10 years of experience in coaching players of all levels. I believe in a holistic approach to training that not only focuses on technique but also on physical and mental strength. My goal is to help you become the best player you can be while enjoying the game.

Services

  • Private Tennis Lessons
  • Group Coaching Sessions
  • Junior Development Programs
  • Fitness and Conditioning Training

Whether you are a beginner looking to learn the basics or an advanced player aiming to refine your skills, I offer personalized coaching tailored to your needs. My sessions are designed to be engaging, effective, and fun. Join me to take your game to the next level!
